juvix’s type system

usages

usage basics

\[ \newcommand\OR{\mathrel|} \newcommand\ORR{\mathrel{\mathrlap{\mkern9mu|}{\hphantom\Coloneqq}}} \]

  • currently usages are either natural numbers or \(ω\) (“any”)
  • a numeric usage is an exact quantity (rather than an upper bound)

\[π, ρ, σ, ζ \Coloneqq n \OR ω \quad (n \in \mathbb{N})\]

operations

  • usages form a semiring

\[ \begin{gathered} ω + π = ω = π + ω \\[0.5em] 0 \, m = 0 = 0 \, n \\ ω \, π = ω = π \, ω \quad (n \ne 0) \end{gathered} \]

subtraction

(inspired by Allais (2018))

  • in fact usages are almost a ring, but with no negatives, subtraction is partial

\[ \begin{gathered} ω - π = ω \quad \text{(including $ω - ω$)} \\ m - n = \mathord⊥ \quad \text{ if } m < n \end{gathered} \]

emptiness

\[ \newcommand\Rule[3]{ \frac{\begin{gathered}#2\end{gathered}}{#3}\:[\textsf{#1}] } \newcommand\Empty{\: \mathbf{empty}} \]

  • if we’re tracking usages exactly, then it should have zero left when it goes out of scope
  • otherwise it stays \(ω\) the whole time so that counts as “empty” too
  • in rules, usages are called \(ζ\) when they should be empty

\[ \Rule{e-zero}{}{0 \Empty} \qquad \Rule{e-any}{}{ω \Empty} \]

current types

type rules (most of them)

\[ \newcommand\Ty[4]{ \IN{\begin{smallmatrix}#1\\#2\end{smallmatrix}} ⊢ #3 ⊣ \OUT{#4} } \newcommand\TyZ[2]{\IN{#1} ⊢_0 #2} \newcommand\IN{\textcolor{Apricot}} \newcommand\OUT{\textcolor{SpringGreen}} \newcommand\SYN{\textcolor{Lavender}{⇒}} \newcommand\CHK{\textcolor{SkyBlue}{⇐}} \newcommand\Syn[3]{\IN{#1} · \IN{#2} \SYN \OUT{#3}} \newcommand\Chk[3]{\IN{#1} · \IN{#2} \CHK \IN{#3}} \newcommand\ChkZ[2]{\IN{#1} \CHK \IN{#2}} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} A, B, C, s, t & \Coloneqq e \OR \dotsb & \text{terms} \\ e, f & \Coloneqq \dotsb & \text{eliminations} \\[.5em] Γ & \Coloneqq • \OR Γ, x : A & \text{type contexts} \\ Θ & \Coloneqq • \OR Θ, π · x & \text{usage contexts} \\[.5em] \mathcal{J} & \Coloneqq \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk σ t A}{Θ'} & \text{judgements} \\ & \ORR \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn σ e A}{Θ'} \\ & \ORR \IN ζ \Empty \end{aligned} \]

  • orange is input, green is output
  • \(\CHK\) is checking, \(\SYN\) is synthesis
  • \(\TyZ{\IN Γ}{\ChkZ s A}\) is an abbreviation for \(\Ty{\IN Γ}{\IN Θ}{\Chk 0 s A}{\OUT Θ}\) for some (irrelevant) \(Θ\)
  • terms are constructions, which can be checked against an input type (\(\CHK\))
  • eliminations are spines of eliminators (application, projection, etc) applied to a stuck term like a variable (\(\SYN\))

universes, variables

\[ \begin{gathered} \begin{aligned} i &\in \mathbb{N} \\ A &\Coloneqq \dotsb \OR \star_i \OR x \end{aligned} \\[1em] % \Rule{star}{\IN i < \IN j}{\TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{\star_i}{\star_j}}} \\[.5em] \Rule{var}{}{ \Ty{Γ₁, & x : A, & Γ₂}{Θ₁, & π · x, & Θ₂}{\Syn σ x A}{Θ₁, π-σ, Θ₂} } \end{gathered} \]

primitives

\[ \newcommand\M{\mathbfsf} \]

  • \(P\) ranges over primitive types, \(p\) over primitive values
  • \(\M{hasType}\) and \(\M{arity}\) are defined by the parameterisation

\[ \begin{aligned} t &\Coloneqq \dotsb \OR P \OR p \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{gathered} \Rule{prim}{\M{hasType}(\IN p, \IN A)}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk σ p A}{Θ} } \\[1em] \Rule{primty}{\M{arity}(\IN P) = \OUT k}{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{P}{\underbrace{\star_0 \to \dotsb}_k \to \star_0}} } \end{gathered} \]

function & [dependent] pair types

\[ \newcommand\Pair[1]{\langle #1 \rangle} \]

\[ \begin{gathered} A \Coloneqq \dotsb \OR Π(π·x: A). B \OR λx. t \OR \Pair{s, t} \\[1em] % \Rule{fun}{% \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}} \qquad \TyZ{Γ, x: A}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}} }{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{Π(π·x: A). B}{\star_i}} }\\[1em] \Rule{pair}{% \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}} \qquad \TyZ{Γ, x: A}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}} }{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{Σ(π·x: A). B}{\star_i}} } \end{gathered} \]

abstraction, application

\[ \begin{gathered} \Rule{abs}{% \Ty{Γ, & x: A}{Θ, & σπ·x}{\Chk σ t B}{Θ', ζ·x} \qquad \IN ζ \: \Empty }{% \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{λx. t}{Π(π·x: A). B}}{Θ'}% }\\[1em] \Rule{app}{% \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn{σ}{f}{Π(π·x: A). B}}{Θ'} \\ \Ty{Γ}{Θ'}{\Chk{σπ}{s}{A}}{Θ''}% }{% \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn{σ}{f \: s}{B[x \coloneqq s]}}{Θ''}% } \end{gathered} \]

dependent pair construction

\[ \Rule{cons}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{s}{A}}{Θ'} \\ \Ty{Γ}{Θ'}{\Chk{σ}{t}{B[x \coloneqq s]}}{Θ''} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\Pair{s,t}}{Σ(x: A). B}}{Θ''} } \]

(no builtin eliminator for \(Σ\) at the moment)

categorical product

\[ \newcommand\CPair[1]{⟪#1⟫} \newcommand\K\mathsf \newcommand\Fst[1]{\K{fst}_{#1}\:} \newcommand\Snd[1]{\K{snd}_{#1}\:} \]

\[ A, t \Coloneqq \dotsb \OR A \times B \OR \CPair{s,t} \OR \Fst{B} s \OR \Snd{A} s \]

\[ \begin{gathered} \Rule{cpair}{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}} \qquad \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}} }{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A \times B}{\star_i}} } \\[1em] \Rule{ccons}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{s}{A}}{Θ'} \qquad \Ty{Γ}{Θ'}{\Chk{σ}{t}{B}}{Θ''} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\CPair{s,t}}{A \times B}}{Θ''} } \end{gathered} \]

categorical product projections

\[ \begin{gathered} \Rule{cfst}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}}{Θ} \qquad \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{s}{A \times B}}{Θ'} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\Fst{B} s}{A}}{Θ'} } \\[1em] \Rule{csnd}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}}{Θ} \qquad \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{s}{A \times B}}{Θ'} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\Snd{A} s}{B}}{Θ'} } \end{gathered} \]

categorical coproduct construction

\[ \newcommand\Inl{\K{inl}\:} \newcommand\Inr{\K{inr}\:} \newcommand\Case[1]{\K{case}_{#1}\:} \]

\[ A, t \Coloneqq \dotsb \OR A+B \OR \Inl s \OR \Inr t \OR \Case{A+B} s \: t_1 \: t_2 \]

\[ \begin{gathered} \Rule{csum}{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}} \qquad \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}} }{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A + B}{\star_i}} } \\[1em] \Rule{cinl}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{s}{A}}{Θ'} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\Inl s}{A + B}}{Θ'} } \\[1em] \Rule{cinr}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{t}{B}}{Θ'} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\Inr t}{A + B}}{Θ'} } \end{gathered} \]

categorical coproduct elimination

\[ \Rule{ccase}{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}} \qquad \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}} \qquad \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{s}{A+B}}{Θ'} \\ \Ty{Γ}{Θ'}{\Chk{σ}{t_1}{A →_π C}}{Θ''} \qquad \Ty{Γ}{Θ'}{\Chk{σ}{t_2}{B →_π C}}{Θ''} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\Case{A+B} s \: t_1 \: t_2}{C}}{Θ''} } \]

  • both branches \(t_1,t_2\) have to have the same usage input/output because only one of them is used

local binding

\[ \newcommand\Let{\K{let}\:} \newcommand\In{\:\K{in}\:} \]

\[ t \Coloneqq \dotsb \OR \Let π · x = e \In t \]

\[ \Rule{let}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn{σπ}{e}{B}}{Θ'} \\ \Ty{Γ,&x:A}{Θ',&π·x}{\Chk{σ}{t}{A}}{Θ''} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{(\Let π · x = e \In t)}{A}}{Θ''} } \]

subtyping

\[ \mathcal{J} \Coloneqq \dotsb \OR \IN π \preceq \IN ρ \OR \IN s <: \IN t \]

\[ \begin{gathered} \Rule{sub-refl}{}{\IN π \preceq \IN π} \qquad \Rule{sub-any}{}{\IN π \preceq \IN ω} \\[1em] \Rule{sub-star}{\IN i \le \IN j}{\IN{\star_i} <: \IN{\star_j}} \\[.5em] \Rule{sub-pi}{ \IN{π₁} \preceq \IN{π₂} \qquad \IN{A₂} <: \IN{A₁} \qquad \IN{B₁} <: \IN{B₂} }{ \IN{Π(π₁ · x: A₁). B₁} <: \IN{Π(π₂ · x: A₂). B₂} } \\[1em] \Rule{sub-sig}{ \IN{π₁} \preceq \IN{π₂} \qquad \IN{A₁} <: \IN{A₂} \qquad \IN{B₁} <: \IN{B₂} }{ \IN{Σ(π₁ · x: A₁). B₁} <: \IN{Σ(π₂ · x: A₂). B₂} } \end{gathered} \]

  • Π is contravariant is its domain, but Σ is covariant in both components

applying subtyping

\[ \Rule{sub}{% \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn σ e A}{Θ'} \qquad \IN A <: \IN{A'} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk σ e {A'}}{Θ'} } \]

first class usages

\[ \newcommand\Usage{\K{Usage}} \newcommand\Any{\K{Any}} \]

  • new builtins:
    • \(\Usage: \star_0\)
    • \(0, 1, \dotsc, \Any : \Usage\)
    • \(\mathord+, \mathord· : \Usage →_1 \Usage →_1 \Usage\)
  • no eliminator; usages are totally opaque
  • can compute with them by representing them as e.g. Maybe Nat and interpreting them to actual \(\Usage\) values just before use
  • the ring equations that arise can be solved automatically

\[ \Rule{fun}{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{π}{\Usage}} \qquad \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}} \qquad \TyZ{Γ, x: A}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}} }{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{Π(π·x: A). B}{\star_i}} } \]

move coproduct eliminator to elimination category

\[ \newcommand\CaseE[1]{\K{case}_{#1}\:} \]

  • \(\K{case}\) can be moved to Elim
  • its return type will need to be given but it can also become dependent

\[ \Rule{case}{ \TyZ{Γ, x: A+B}{\ChkZ{C}{\star_i}} \\ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn{σ}{e}{A+B}}{Θ'} \\ \Ty{Γ,& y: A}{Θ',& 1·y}{\Chk{σ}{t₁}{C[x \coloneqq \Inl y]}}{Θ'', ζ·x} \\ \Ty{Γ,& y: B}{Θ',& 1·y}{\Chk{σ}{t₂}{C[x \coloneqq \Inr y]}}{Θ'', ζ·x} \\ \IN ζ \Empty }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn{σ}{\CaseE{x.C} e \: (y. t₁) \: (y. t₂)}{C[x \coloneqq e]}}{Θ''} } \]

\[ \newcommand\Box[1]{\square_{#1}\:} \newcommand\Pack{\K{pack}\:} \newcommand\Unpack[1]{\K{unpack}_{#1}\:} \]

\[ \begin{gathered} \Rule{box}{ (\TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{π}{\Usage}}) \qquad \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{A}{\star_i}} }{ \TyZ{Γ}{\ChkZ{\Box π A}{\star_i}} } \\[1em] \Rule{pack}{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σπ}{s}{A}}{Θ'} }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Chk{σ}{\Pack s}{\Box π A}}{Θ'} } \\[1em] \Rule{unpack}{ \TyZ{Γ, x: A}{\ChkZ{B}{\star_i}} \qquad \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn{σ}{e}{\Box π A}}{Θ'} \\ \Ty{Γ,& y: A}{Θ',& π · y}{\Chk{σ}{t}{B[x \coloneqq \Pack y]}}{Θ'', ζ·y} \qquad \IN ζ \Empty }{ \Ty{Γ}{Θ}{\Syn{σ}{\Unpack{x.B} x = e \In t}{B[x \coloneqq e]}}{Θ''} } \end{gathered} \]
